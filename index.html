About Us

Forn w Saj Al Day3a is a family-owned bakery dedicated to preserving traditional bread-making techniques while incorporating modern baking standards.

Our name, which translates to "Oven and Saj of the Village," reflects our commitment to authentic preparation methods. We source our ingredients from local farmers and millers whenever possible, supporting our community while ensuring the highest quality.

Every morning before dawn, we begin our work, combining time-honored techniques with a passion for creating exceptional baked goods. From our signature saj bread to our delicate manakeesh, each item is made with care and attention to detail.

What started as a small neighborhood bakery has grown into a beloved destination for those seeking authentic flavors and artisanal quality. Yet we remain true to our roots, with the same commitment to quality and hospitality that marked our humble beginnings.
